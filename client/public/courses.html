Are courses free?

Pricing varies. Most courses are free, though you may need to purchase additional study material to participate. We have made every effort to keep these expenses to a minimum so our study options remain widely accessible.

Please view our courses for specific details.

What type of courses do you provide?

We facilitate self-study courses in the following formats:

  • Online (access the course via our online Study Portal)
  • Correspondence (conducted via post)
  • Subscription (study material is purchased based on a monthly or annual subscription plan)
  • Multimedia (an interactive learning experience combining reading and video contents)

Please view our courses for specific details.

Are more courses available?

New courses are published periodically and facilitated throughout the year.

Do I need to attend virtual online classes?

No. Our flexible self-study courses let you learn at a time and place that suits you.

Where do I go to complete my online course?

All online courses are available via our Study Portal which you can access at any time, day or night, on any smart device.

Do you provide certificates for course completion?

In most instances a certificate is awarded for successful completion of the study. Please view our courses for specific details.

Do I need additional study material?

Study material requirements vary from course to course. Some are mandatory, while others are optional or included as part of the enrolment fee.

Please view our courses for specific details.

What is an Enrolment Key and where do I find it?

Some online courses require an Enrolment Key to access via the Study Portal. In such instances, these details will be provided by email after registering to enrol. Please check your inbox and spam folder for details if your enrolment has been confirmed.

Please view our courses for specific details and contact us if you need further assistance.

Note: You only need to enter the Enrolment Key once per course.

What if I have more questions?

Please contact us for further information and assistance.
