Need Prayer?

We’d love to pray for you.

Whatever your need, prayer is the answer. Your confidential prayer request will be forwarded to the appropriate National Office prayer team who will be seeking God and interceding for you.

Privacy

Your prayer request is a sacred trust and is kept confidential. To help us comply with data protection regulations please refer to any person(s) by their first name only and omit all other identifiable information (i.e. surname, contact details, geographic location, job role etc.).
