Laying The Foundation Series

Whether a building is made of wood, bricks, stone or concrete, the foundations set the limit for any structure, both in terms of size and weight.

The same applies to our Christian faith.

You cannot build a successful Spirit-filled life with a compromised foundation. We must dig deep and discard any beliefs, attitudes, and behaviours that hinder us from knowing Jesus and walking in the fullness of His ways.

What is the state of your foundations? Have you laid the right foundation?

Building a successful Spirit-filled life

Widely regarded as the crown jewel in Derek Prince’s vast Bible teaching archive, the Laying The Foundation series provides a thorough initiation for sound Christian living.

It is designed to help believers progress to spiritual maturity, examining six essential principles of the Christian faith as outlined in Hebrews 6:1-2, which are:

  • Salvation
  • Baptism
  • The Holy Spirit
  • The laying on of hands
  • Resurrection
  • Eternal judgement

A sound understanding and application of these key truths can help believers set a firm foundation for their faith and build a successful life.

Proverbs 25:2 says:

“It is the glory of God to conceal a matter; to search out a matter is the glory of kings.” (NIV)

This passage reminds us of the importance of seeking truth and actively investing in our relationship with our Heavenly Father. Conversely, God will withhold deeper spiritual realities and insights from us until we are ready to receive them. Setting a sound foundation for faith is a key precept for spiritual development and greater understanding.

Formally known as Foundations For Christian Living, the Laying The Foundation series offers timeless Bible-based teaching that will encourage you as you grow in Christ.

Study Formats

Widely referenced by church leaders and Christian educators across the globe, the Laying The Foundation series provides a detailed, yet easy-to-follow study that is available in a wide range of formats.

Books

First published in 1993 under the title The Spirit Filled Believer's Handbook, the series is available as one complete study compilation or ten bitesize individual books.

Individual Books:

  1. Founded On The Rock
  2. Authority And Power Of God’s Word
  3. Through Repentance To Faith
  4. Faith And Works
  5. The Doctrine Of Baptisms
  6. Immersion In The Spirit
  7. Transmitting God’s Power
  8. At The End Of Time
  9. Resurrection Of The Body
  10. Final Judgment

Compilation Books:

  • The Spirit-Filled Believer’s Handbook
  • Foundational Truths for Christian Living
  • Foundations for Righteous Living
  • Foundations for Life
  • Foundation Series

Note: Each book is the same compilation published under different titles.
