The power of prayer

We believe in the power of prayer and a supernatural God who guides, comforts, encourages and blesses all who seek Him. It is an essential component of faith which we apply to every aspect of our ministry.

Our deep desire and heart is to walk with the Lord in everything we do. Psalm 127:1 says:

“Unless the Lord builds the house, the builders labor in vain.” (NIV)

We invite you to partner with us in prayer as we continue to seek God and make disciples of all nations for Christ. By joining us in prayer, you will be supporting our efforts to spread the Good News, teach the Bible, and see the power of God's love transform lives. Your participation will also be a great source of encouragement for us as we continue this important mission.
