IT Support

Remote Access

In order for Derek Prince Digital to provide IT support, we require one-time access to your device.

Provide Access

Instructions

  1. Click the link to download and run a small remote access app (Splashtop SOS). No installation required.
  2. Give the 9-digit number displayed to the Derek Prince Digital team member.
  3. Close the app to end the support session. Derek Prince Digital cannot access this computer until you download and run the app again.
