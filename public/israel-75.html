Join Us In Celebrating Israel’s 75th Anniversary

This year marks Israel's momentous 75th anniversary, and to commemorate this milestone, Derek Prince Ministries presents a comprehensive collection of free Bible teachings on the unique calling of the Jewish people by Derek Prince. If you have ever wondered where we are in God’s end times plan, look at this remarkable nation!

“Israel is God’s prophetic clock. By restoring the people to the land, God has set the clock once again in motion. After long centuries of silence, it is now telling the time.” (Derek Prince - Key to the Middle East)

As a passionate believer in Israel's restoration as part of God’s master plan for the salvation of mankind, Derek Prince highlighted the importance of Israel throughout his ministry. He firmly believed that until the Jewish people enter into their full inheritance, other nations cannot fully enjoy the blessings that God has in store for them.

Through his teachings, Derek Prince emphasizes that the Bible is the most reliable source to understand the unfolding story of Israel and the Middle East, offering unparalleled insight in how they relate to the Church and God’s plan for the end times.

“The Bible makes it abundantly clear that when Jesus comes back, the Jewish people will be in their land and occupying their city, Jerusalem. The enemy of God and man, Satan, is fighting desperately to keep Israel from possessing their land.

This leads to a serious conclusion, that if anybody takes a stand against the Jewish people recovering their land and their city, Jerusalem, he is in fact opposing the purposes of God and the return of Jesus.” (Derek Prince - Israel in the Headlines)
